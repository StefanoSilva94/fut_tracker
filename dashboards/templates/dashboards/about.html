Getting Started

Our Goal

FUT Tracker aims to help bring transparency to pack weight. It allows users to compare odds across different player picks and packs as well offer the chance to view all players that they pack across the game cycle. Some SBCs we know are not always worth the cost, FUT Tracker lets you 'try before you buy'!

How It Works

First create an account on fut-tracker.co.uk so that you can link your email to our Chrome extension
To get the most out of FUT Tracker we recommend downloading our FUT Tracker Chrome extension
This is the tool that will actually track your pack and player pick data. After downloading the extension, click the extension icon and enter the email you used to sign up.

Now start opening packs on the FC25 webapp and view your results live on our My Packs section

Common questions

Do I need to link it to my Ultimate Team account?
No! We will never ask you for your ultimate team login details, the extension works independently in the background of the web app.

Why do I need to sign up, can't I just view the website with making an account?
You do not need to sign up, you can view the home dashboard and see general stats there. However, an account is needed to sync with the extension so that you can track your individual stats
