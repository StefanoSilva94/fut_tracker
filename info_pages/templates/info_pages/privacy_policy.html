Privacy Policy

Fut Tracker Extension

Effective Date: 01/10/2024

At Fut Tracker Extension, your privacy is a top priority. This document explains what data we use, how we use it, and why certain permissions are required to deliver the extension's functionality.

Permissions and Data Usage

  • "Storage" Permission

    Why We Use It: We use local storage to save certain data such as user preferences and sync statuses.

    What It Does: Stores non-personal data such as the user ID for a smoother user experience, allowing you to avoid entering your email multiple times.

  • "Host Permission (ea.com/webapp/*)"

    Why We Use It: Our extension is designed to track gameplay data from EA's web app. This host permission allows us to gather relevant information like pack rates and player picks on EA’s platform.

    What It Does: Enables us to collect information on the web app during your gaming session.

Data Collection

The Fut Tracker Extension does not collect or store any personal data. While we ask for your email to retrieve your user ID, this information is not stored. The email is used only temporarily to communicate with the backend, after which your user ID is stored in your browser's local storage for future reference.

Your Data, Your Control

You can clear the data stored in your browser’s local storage at any time through your browser’s settings. This will remove the user ID stored by the extension.
