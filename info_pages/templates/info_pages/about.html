Getting Started

Our Goal

FUT Tracker aims to help bring transparency to pack weight. It allows users to compare odds across different player picks and packs, as well offer the chance to view all players that they pack across the game cycle.
Some SBCs we know are not always worth the cost, FUT Tracker lets you 'try before you buy'!

Sign Up

Sign up to FUT Tracker using the link (url to be added). Once you have signed up this will allow you to to use our FUT Tracker extension and then view your data on the site.

FUT Tracker Extension

To get the most out of FUT Tracker we recommend installing our FUT Tracker Chrome extension from the Chrome app store after you have create an account on the FUT Tracker webiste. Once downloaded click the extension icon and enter the email you have used to sign up to fut-tracker.co.uk and click Sync Email.

This will track the stats of every pack and player pick you open on the FC 25 web app and the results will be visible on the FUT Tracker website. The data is used to show the odds for different packs and player picks. You can also export your pack data from the My Packs (URL to add) page.

NOTE: No private data is collected or used for the FUT Tracker Chrome extension, only data from the packs and player picks.
